Key Analysis Features

1. Observation Space Visualizer: Selecting an agent overlays their action mask and dispatcher recommendation directly onto the grid. A blue arrow shows the recommended geodesic path, and valid moves from the mask are highlighted in green.

2. Reservation Table: Displays the time-space reservations made by the Heuristic Priority Dispatcher. This allows you to track where trains are planning to yield at switches and corridors.

3. Slack & Priority: Higher priority (lower slack values) trains reserve paths first. You can investigate how slack increases or decreases based on delay and latest arrival timetables.
